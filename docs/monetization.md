# TenderPilot AI - Monetization & Business Strategy

TenderPilot AI is designed to scale commercially as an enterprise-grade SaaS platform.

---

## 1. Target Market
* **Tier 1 (SMEs & Startups)**: Emerging technology companies and contractors looking to bid on public contracts without hiring dedicated proposal writing teams.
* **Tier 2 (Enterprise Operations)**: Major engineering and consulting firms managing hundreds of active bids simultaneously.
* **Tier 3 (Government Contractors)**: Specialized firms bidding on smart city, railway, and municipal tenders.

---

## 2. Pricing Tiers (SaaS Model)

### Starter Edition (SMEs)
* **Pricing**: $1,500/month (billed annually)
* **Features**:
  * Track up to 3 portals.
  * Discovery, Eligibility, and Planner agents active.
  * Standard Microsoft Graph connections (Outlook notification drafts).
  * Up to 50 portal crawls/month.

### Enterprise Professional
* **Pricing**: $4,500/month (billed annually)
* **Features**:
  * Track up to 15 portals.
  * All 8 agents active (including Competitor Intelligence and Reflection agents).
  * Custom Cosmos DB Knowledge Graph memory database.
  * High-frequency crawling (daily).
  * Priority Microsoft Planner and Jira task creations.
  * Multi-user approval flows.

### Dedicated Sovereign (Enterprise Private Cloud)
* **Pricing**: Custom contract (Starting at $9,500/month)
* **Features**:
  * Unlimited portals.
  * Deployable inside client's Azure Tenant.
  * Custom Bicep orchestration templates.
  * Direct integration with internal Microsoft Graph environments.

---

## 3. Distribution Channels
* **Microsoft Azure Marketplace**: Partnering with Microsoft enterprise account managers to sell TenderPilot directly into MS Office 365 enterprise customers.
* **BD Procurement Analytics ROI**: Show how substituting manual tender hunting with TenderPilot's 91% Autonomy Score saves enterprises up to $180,000/year in operational BD overhead.
